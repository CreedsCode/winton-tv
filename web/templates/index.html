How it works

  1. 1

    Join the Winton Discord

    Streaming and chat are gated to verified guild members. Anyone can watch.

  2. 2

    Sign in & pick your channel

    Login with Discord, choose your channel name (e.g. winton.tv/yourname), grab your stream key.

  3. 3

    Stream from OBS

    Paste the WHIP URL into OBS. Hit Start Streaming. Sub-500ms latency to viewers.
